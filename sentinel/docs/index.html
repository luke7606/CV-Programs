Risk Assessment

This is a common phishing technique targeting corporate credentials. The link in the email leads to a fake login page designed to steal your Office 365 username and password. If compromised, attackers could access your email, files, and potentially other corporate systems.

Recommended Actions

  • Do not click any links in the email
  • Report the email as phishing to your IT department
  • Delete the email from your inbox
  • If you already clicked the link or entered credentials, change your password immediately
